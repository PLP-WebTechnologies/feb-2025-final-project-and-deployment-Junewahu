Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and more.

How do I book an appointment?

Booking with Drip Mtaani is simple and convenient. You can book through our website, call us directly, or send a WhatsApp message. Our team will confirm your appointment within 30 minutes and provide all necessary details for your visit.

What are your operating hours?

We operate from 8 AM to 10 PM EAT, seven days a week, including holidays. This extended schedule ensures we can accommodate your needs at your convenience, whether it's early morning or late evening.

How long does the service take?

Most treatments take 30-45 minutes to administer, depending on the package selected. Our experienced medical professionals ensure a comfortable and efficient process, with minimal disruption to your schedule.

Do you accept insurance?

Currently, we don't accept insurance. All services are paid for out of pocket. We offer competitive pricing and various payment options to ensure our premium hydration services are accessible to everyone.
