Our Story

Founded in 2025, Drip Mtaani emerged from a simple observation: healthcare should be accessible, convenient, and comfortable. We noticed that many people in Nairobi were struggling with dehydration, hangovers, and general wellness issues, but the traditional healthcare system wasn't meeting their needs.
